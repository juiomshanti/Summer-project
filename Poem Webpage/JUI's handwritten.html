No Man Today

- A Tribute to Lockdown

Today I see this deserted world,
that you so profoundly created.
Today,yellow shines a little brighter,
feathers fly a little higher,
green stands a little taller.
Today,I see blue above & below,
a breath so sacred.
Tranquility stole a piece,
from thy fabric of time.
To witness under a man's reign,
its own sweet chime.
Oh mighty Man!
Today I see No Man.


~Jui Patel
